# Biryani Techniques: Layering Flavours

Understanding how to layer rice, meat and spices is essential. Chef Sopu shares his approach to marination times, rice parboiling, and the dum process that seals the aroma.

- Parboil basmati to the exact texture
- Marinate overnight for richer taste
- Seal the pot and slow-cook for fragrant results

![Sopu at Work](/images/blogdetail-1.jpg)

These techniques turn good ingredients into memorable biryanis. Try them at home or visit our kitchen to taste the original.
